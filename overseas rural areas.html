海外偏鄉義診

Free Medical Services in Overseas Rural Areas

自 2017 年 1 月起, 由本會創會醫師志工團隊, 以及由柬埔寨當地醫學院學生志工等, 首 度到柬埔寨柬國磅士卑省及波蘿勉省的偏鄉區域進行義診,白米物資發放給當地弱勢團 體以及祈福文化交流. 二天的偏鄉義診任務, 來診人次超過五百位, 可謂盛況空前, 這是 在本會成立前, 為提升柬埔寨醫療水平, 偏鄉義診, 人道救援等之平台展開序幕.
Since Jan.2017, several founders of APCMGU as medical doctors & volunteers team in addition to Cambodian medical students volunteers had started to offer the 1st free medical services, rice/daily necessities distributions for 2 days to local vulnerable group, and Prayers of Cultural Exchanges at Kampong Speu & Prey Veng provinces, Cambodia. There were more than 500 local residents who came to the missions for consultations. It was such an unprecedented event as a good beginning to help improve Cambodian medical standard, offer medical services and humanity aid in rural areas of Cambodia.

本會接著陸續從 2017 年至 2018 年 8 月為止, 在數位優秀醫師志工們與柬埔寨當地醫 學院學生志工持續不斷努力支持與熱情付出下, 分別與當地 NGO 或私立醫學院合辦數 十場等的偏鄉義診如下, 這份大愛將不斷延續下去…
Since 2017 to present (Aug. 2018), APCMGU has kept working on co-organizing several free medical services in rural areas with Cambodian local NGO or private medical schools. All are under the team-work of APCMGU doctors & Cambodian medical students volunteers heartily support and contribution, and this Great Love will last forever…
