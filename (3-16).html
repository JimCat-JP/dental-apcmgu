  • 2018.08.21
  • 海外偏鄉義診

2018.08.21 感謝本會第一醫療團隊每位醫師志工們無私的愛心與奉獻，順利完成任務返台。
Highly appreciate APCMGU’s 1st Medical Team for all doctors and volunteers’ unconditional love and contributions. They have completed various missions successfully and returned to Taiwan on 2018.08.21.

接著由台北醫學大學崇德社，北京大學醫學部和本醫學學會合作，包括柬國醫學生志工，台灣志工及一位特地由越南來參與義診的巫藥師，連車帶人坐渡船渡過湄公河，繼續完成第四站與柬國電信部合辦之大型義診，地點在干丹省偏鄉醫療站，是個難能可貴的體驗。
Thereafter, Chong-De Volunteer Club of Taipei Medical University (TMU), Peking University Health Science Center (PUHSC), APCMGU & CCMA, including Cambodian Medical students & Taiwanese volunteers, especially a Pharmacist Wu who came far away from Vietnam to join the missions, all worked together to complete the 4th big mission co-organized with Cambodian Telecom Ministry. Before reaching destination at KOH KHSACH TUNLEA HEALTH CENTER, Saang Dist., Kandal Province, all volunteers including the vehicles need to take the ferry to get across the Meigong River, which was really an unforgettable experience.

雖然看診村民人數眾多，共計 469 看診人次，但是每位醫師用心提供專業看診，所有醫護志工，不分國籍，不分你我，有條不紊共同協助完成此次任務，圓滿喜樂。
Even there were many local villagers about 469 persons coming for medical consultations, but all doctors offered their professional prescriptions, and all volunteers did their fullest to assist to complete this mission impartially regardless of nationality and genders. All were happy and filled with joys!
