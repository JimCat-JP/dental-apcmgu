* 與婦女部合辦
* Co-organized with Women Ministry Khan Dangkor, Phnom Penh

  • 2018.08.19
  • 海外偏鄉義診

第二站 2018.08.19 義診與柬國婦女事務部合辦，地點在金邊郊區小學。除了此次醫療團隊醫師群，與台柬學會志工外，柬國婦女事務部部長更親自到場，帶領該部志工們共同合作，服務約上百位該區民眾, 仁風義舉，傳為美談。

The 2nd free medical mission on 2018.08.19 was co-organized with Ministry of Women’s Affairs (MOWA) at Tien Primary and Secondary School, Sangkat Tien, Khan Dangkor, PP, Cambodia. In addition to Medical Team’s doctors and volunteers from both APCMGU & CCMA, most of all, the minister of Ministry of Women’s Affairs (MOWA) Dr.ING Kantha Phavi especially guided their volunteers, including Deputy Director General of Gender Equality & Economic Development, Mr. The Chhun Hak to work together for the medical mission serving more than hundreds of local residents. Such unconditional love is highly appreciated.
